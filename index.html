About Me
 

My name is Cameron Ythan King. I attend school at Little Red Schoolhouse and Elisabeth Irwin High School (LREI) , located in SoHo, New York. I am a Prep for Prep and All Star Code alumn. I live in Brooklyn, New York. I consider myself an athelte, a musician, a comedian, an artist, a designer and I am an aspiring Computer Engineer.

I recently represented LREI at the White Privilege Conference in Louisville, Kentucky and help led a Black Lives Matter protest with my school. This summer, I participated in New York City's "Ladders for Leaders" program. I am an intern at Bleu Magazine, a men's fashion magazine.
